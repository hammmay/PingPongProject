The Ping-Pong Game

Rules To Play

Ping Pong will count all your numbers up to the number you entered, but with 4 exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 become "pingpong"
  4. Ping Pong doesn't like negative numbers and 0s, so type carefully...
